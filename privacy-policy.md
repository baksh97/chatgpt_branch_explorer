# Privacy Policy — ChatGPT Branch Explorer

**Last updated:** March 29, 2026

This policy describes how the **ChatGPT Branch Explorer** browser extension (“the extension”) handles information when you use it on ChatGPT.

## What the extension does

The extension adds a sidebar and related controls on `chatgpt.com` and `chat.openai.com` to help you see and navigate conversation branches. It reads the structure of the page you already have open to build that view.

## Data we collect

The extension does **not** send your conversation content, browsing history, or personal identifiers to the extension developer’s servers. There is no analytics, advertising, or telemetry in the extension for that purpose.

## Where processing happens

Branch information is derived locally in your browser from ChatGPT’s page. Your chats continue to be handled under OpenAI’s terms and privacy policy when you use ChatGPT.

## Permissions

- **Host access (ChatGPT)** — Required to inject the sidebar and read only what is needed to show branches on those sites.
- **Storage** — May be used to keep small preferences or UI state on your device only. It is not used to upload your data to us.
- **Active tab** — Used so the toolbar action can interact with the current ChatGPT tab.

## Changes

If the extension’s data practices change materially, this policy will be updated and, where required, the extension listing will reflect the new policy.

## Contact

For privacy questions about this extension, contact the developer through the support channel listed on the extension’s store page or repository.
